High Sidin': Hyphy Edition

Download now on Android!

HighSidin’: Hyphy Edition aims to represent the culture of the Bay Area through a competitive dance battle game that utilizes dance moves that are native to Oakland. The world of HighSidin’ puts a legalized twist on a classic sideshow, an illegal event where people perform or watch illegal automobile performances. HighSidin’ imagines an organized league where crews utilize their skills to outperform their opponent and represent their crew in the National Sideshow League (NSL).

Worked on game systems, mechanics and Design tools in Unity for the project. Also Worked on Mobile UI, Porting and Publishing

Tools Used:

  • Unity
  • C#
  • Android Studio
  • Java
